Murphy Cooper

Description

Murphy Cooper is the charater in movie Interstellar. Murphy's mother passed away a long time ago, and her most dependent father went on a voyage to save humanity when she was very young. But instead of wasting her life missing her father, she followed Dr.Brand as a physicist and helped him to solve the gravity problem that has plagued him all his life. In the end, with the efforts of Murphy and her father, humanity has successfully arrived at the space station and is going to sail to a new habitable planet.
